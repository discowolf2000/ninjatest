Features

Powerful, Secure, and Customizable: Everything You Need for HIPAA-Compliant Forms

HIPAA Compliance

  • End-to-End Data Encryption: Safeguard sensitive information with encryption during data transfer and storage.
  • Access Management: Restrict access to sensitive form data to authorized personnel using role-based permissions.
  • Activity Tracking and Audit Logs: Maintain detailed records of who accessed data and when, ensuring full transparency.
  • Secure Dashboard: Manage and view form submissions through a private, encrypted dashboard.
  • Compliance Maintenance: Regular updates to ensure your forms stay aligned with evolving HIPAA regulations.

Insights and Reporting

  • Submission Analytics: Monitor submission rates, user engagement, and completion trends.
  • Filterable Responses: Sort and filter submissions by date, field values, or custom tags for easier analysis.
  • Compliance Reports: Generate detailed reports to demonstrate compliance during audits or reviews.

Integrations and Extensions

  • HIPAA-Compliant API: Connect forms to your existing systems securely.
  • Third-Party Tool Integration: Sync with popular tools like Google Sheets, HIPAA-compliant cloud storage, or EMR/EHR systems.
  • Payment Collection: Accept secure payments for services, co-pays, or fees directly through your forms.

Form Creation and Customization

  • Advanced Field Types: Add dropdowns, checkboxes, star ratings, file uploads, date pickers, and more to collect precise data.
  • Conditional Logic: Customize the user experience by showing or hiding fields based on previous responses.
  • Multi-Step Forms: Break down lengthy forms into manageable sections for improved user experience.
  • Custom Styling: Tailor forms to match your branding with adjustable colors, fonts, layouts, and logos.
  • Reusable Templates: Choose from a variety of pre-designed templates tailored for healthcare, dentistry, and therapy, ready to customize.
  • Multi-Language Support: Create forms in multiple languages to reach a broader audience.

Automation and Notifications

  • Instant Alerts: Get real-time notifications when a form is submitted, so you can act immediately.
  • Custom Email Notifications: Send secure and customizable notifications to administrators and users after form submission.
  • Autoresponder Emails: Automatically send acknowledgment emails with customizable content to form submitters.

Advanced Data Management

  • Secure File Uploads: Accept sensitive documents like medical records or signed forms with secure file upload functionality.
  • Data Retention Controls: Configure data retention policies to automatically delete old data in line with HIPAA regulations.
  • Export and Integration: Easily export responses in CSV or integrate them with EMR/EHR systems, CRMs, and other HIPAA-compliant tools.
  • Offline Mode: Allow users to complete forms without an internet connection, syncing securely once reconnected.
  • Submission Analytics: Track key metrics like submission rates, response times, and abandonment trends.

User Experience Enhancements

  • Mobile-Friendly Design: Forms are fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop use.
  • Post-Submission Options: Redirect users to a thank-you page, display custom messages, or trigger follow-up actions after submission.
  • Save and Resume Later: Enable users to save partially completed forms and return to finish them later.
  • Captcha and Anti-Spam: Keep your forms secure with built-in spam prevention tools.

Support and Resources

  • Onboarding Support: Comprehensive guidance for setting up and optimizing your HIPAA-compliant forms.
  • Help Center and Tutorials: Access detailed documentation, FAQs, and video guides.
  • Dedicated Customer Support: 24/7 assistance via chat, email, or phone to address your needs.
